## Post Requirements (per SEO guide)

- Unique `<title>` (50-60 chars, keyword first)
- `<meta name="description">` (120-155 chars)
- FAQ schema (3+ Q&A per post)
- Internal links to related posts + coin tracker
- CTA: "Track these coins in real-time on xCrypto24.com"
- Word count: Tier 1: 300-500w, Tier 2-3: 800-1500w, Tier 4-5: 1500w+
